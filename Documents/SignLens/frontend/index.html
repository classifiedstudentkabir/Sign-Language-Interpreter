Permissions Required

How to Enable Camera & Microphone

1

Check Browser Permissions

When prompted, click "Allow" for both camera and microphone access. If you accidentally clicked "Block", you'll need to reset permissions in your browser settings.

2

Browser Settings

Go to your browser settings → Privacy and Security → Site Settings → Camera/Microphone. Find "SIGN A.I INTERPRETER" and change the permission to "Allow".

3

Device Permissions

Make sure your device allows camera and microphone access at the system level. Check your operating system's privacy settings.

4

Hardware Check

Ensure your camera and microphone are properly connected and not being used by another application.
